🌿 Bhumia.com

Building the Future of Land Registration – Fully Online, Transparent, and Corruption-Free

🌍 What is Bhumia?

Bhumia.com is an open-source initiative aiming to build an undefeatable, fully online, government-compliant system for registering lands, plots, and farms, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and accessibility for all.

🚀 Our Vision

Currently, we do not have code, but we have a strong vision. We are building a team of contributors, civic technologists, and domain experts to design, develop, and launch a prototype together for a transparent land registration system.

🛠 Prototype Goals

  • Online land and plot registration workflow with verification
  • Online payment handling for stamp duties and fees
  • Immutable record-keeping with blockchain readiness
  • Searchable public land registry with privacy layers
  • Integration with GIS and government APIs
  • Dashboards for citizens, surveyors, and officers
  • Mobile-first, multilingual design for accessibility
  • Clear audit trails for accountability

🌱 Why Is This Important?

  • Over 70% of disputes in many countries are land-related
  • Land fraud causes billions in losses globally each year
  • Current systems are often opaque, manual, and corruption-prone
  • Open, transparent systems can empower citizens and governments

Bhumia.com is not just a software project; it is a movement for systemic transparency in land governance.
